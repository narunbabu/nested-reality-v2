### 2. Structured Data (JSON-LD Schema)

**Implemented Schemas:**
- **Book Schema**: Complete book information with pricing, ISBN, reviews, ratings
- **Organization Schema**: Publisher and brand information
- **Article Schema**: For blog posts and reviews (dynamic)

**Benefits:**
- Rich snippets in Google search results
- Enhanced visibility with star ratings
- Price display in search results
- Author information in knowledge panels

### 3. Page-Specific SEO Optimization

#### Homepage (`/`)
- **Title**: "Nested Reality - Revolutionary Physics Book | Density-Based Theory of Everything"
- **Focus**: Broad appeal, high-volume physics keywords
- **CTAs**: Direct links to Amazon, reviews, concept explorer

#### Reviews Page (`/reviews`)
- **Title**: "Reviews - AI Analysis & Reader Testimonials | Nested Reality Physics Book"
- **Focus**: Comparison keywords (vs General Relativity, vs Quantum Field Theory, vs Dark Matter)
- **Content**: AI-generated comprehensive analysis + human reviews
-**SEO Value**: Rich review content attracts "book review" searches

#### Concept Explorer (`/explorer`)
- **Title**: "Concept Explorer - Interactive Physics Concepts | Nested Reality Theory"
- **Focus**: Educational keywords, interactive learning, AI assistant
- **Content**: Interactive Q&A, concept explanations

#### About Book (`/about-book`)
- **Title**: "About the Book - Nested Reality | Density-Based Physics Theory Explained"
- **Focus**: Purchase intent keywords, book synopsis
- **Content**: Full book description, table of contents, purchase links

#### About Author (`/about-author`)
- **Title**: "About Arun Nalamara - Physicist & Author | Nested Reality Theory"
- **Focus**: Author name, physicist credentials
- **SEO Value**: Builds author authority, E-A-T signals

#### Blog (`/blog`)
- **Title**: "Physics Blog - Articles on Density Theory & Quantum Mechanics"
- **Focus**: Educational content, physics articles
- **SEO Value**: Regular fresh content, topic authority

### 4. Technical SEO

#### Sitemap (`/sitemap.xml`)
- Automated XML sitemap
- Priority weighting: Homepage (1.0), Explorer/About Book (0.9), Reviews (0.8)
- Change frequency optimization

#### Robots.txt (`/robots.txt`)
- Allows all search engines
- Blocks admin/dashboard areas
- Points to sitemap

#### Meta Tags
- Open Graph (Facebook, LinkedIn)
- Twitter Cards (large image summaries)
- Canonical URLs (prevent duplicate content)
- Mobile optimization tags
